About The Game

What is Ragnarok Origin Classic all about?

ROOC or Ragnarok Origin Classic is an MMORPG where adventurers experiences the unique features that this game has. The dungeons, Main and Side Quests, Teaming-Up with other players and explore various types of classes that fits to their playstyle!

Gameplay

Players starts as a newbie or a novice in the game. They kill mobs to gain Exp and Job. Exp is the main levelling point of the game or the Base Level. Job also levels up but it allows you to unlock the classes that you chose and unleash their full potential. Walking through the Game Story, Dungeons, Events and Daily Commissions will also help a starter to level up.
