My Personal Reviews on ROOC

For me, as someone who is a fan of MMORPG games, this game is the only one that never made me quit. With the way how beginner-friendly this game is, following by its enjoyable events and helpful players also, I can say that this game is enjoyable in long-term experience, even if sometimes it feels too repetitive, the developers and company of this game always gives their best to improve the game mechanics to their players so that they won't get bored and tired of the game in the long run, and I believe they did it well. With the way on how you can play without moving a penny in your pocket, the different classes that you can use, and its welcoming community too, honestly thats what makes me still play this game.
