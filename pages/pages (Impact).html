What is the Impact of ROOC?

Key Impact of the Game:

Ragnarok Origin Classic offers more of a Free2Play friendly among the adventurers. Compared to its global version, where every currency needs to be paid using real money because of its power system, Ragnarok Origin Classic beats this dilemma because it is centralized more on farming, levelling-up your own character using your own skills, rather than purchasing currency just to be more powerful. As of now, Ragnarok Origin Classic is only playable and popular amongst the SEA Region.
