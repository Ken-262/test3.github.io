Classes Overview:

Swordsman

This class excels more at having more hp and physical defense compared to other classes. They can absorb massive damage while also dealing a consistent amount of damage to mobs.

Mage

Despite having a low defense on their survivability, this class' upperhand is the ability of it to deal damage by having a huge AoE (Area of Effect) on their skillset, killing mobs or enemies from a distance.

Archer

This class excels in range and mobility where they use bow and elemental arrows. They use elemental arrows based on the type of mobs that they are killing.

Acolyte

This class excels more on expanding the teammates' survivability. They use buffs to support their teammates and uses holy magic to deal damage and weaken the undead or demon mobs.

Thief

This class is more of an agile and mobile character. This one excels in positioning, stealth, and high critical damages to kill the mobs quickly.
